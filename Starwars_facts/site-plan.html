Overview

Purpose

Have you been curious about a character in Star-Wars? They being clouded in mystery or their information is just hard to find? I aim to create a site that will bring those extra details to light in a way that is easy to find and understand. With the new shows that are coming out now, it leaves new viewers wondering where these characters came from and why they are important. I am a hardcore Star-Wars fan, so I have viewed and read almost everything about it. Which has allowed me to learn many details that others wouldn’t know. I will categorize each character onto the site and put those stories and details I have learned there, so others can learn and appreciate a deeper look into the Star-Wars universe.

Audience

With there being so many new shows out now, there are many people who may be new to the Star Wars universe. It can be daunting to try and learn about a particular character without watching everything they have shown up in. This site is aimed to those people. Instead of watching everything, they can instead look up the character and all of their information is in one place, making it easier to understand them. And by so doing, grants them a greater appreciation of the characters and their stories.

Content

Home page

People will be curious as to the backstory of various characters, they will be looking for information on them. This site is a one stop place for all these characters and their deeper details that may not be widely known. It can get confusing where all these characters belong in this universe, and who they fight for. They have been organized in their factions according to the eras they belong to, to more understandable.

Images for the Home page

Era And Faction

From the events of “The Fantom Menace” to the end of “Rise of Sky Walker” is a time span of 67 years. There are 3 major conflicts that span this time. The Old Republic vs The Separatists, The Rebellion against the Empire and The First Order against the Resistance. All of these characters can fall into one of these factions, whether they fought for the light side, or for the dark side.

Images for the Page 2

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-wan Kenobi. Jedi master and member of the Jedi High counsel, master to Anakin Skywalker, apprentice to Qui-gon Jin, general for the republic during the Clone Wars and mentor to Luke Skywalker. Obi-wan Kenobi the wise and stoic man of honor and upholder of the Jedi code has been involved in many of the major conflicts seen throughout Star Wars, most of which took place during the clone wars of which he was a commanding general of the clone army. He also held a seat on the Jedi high counsel with other Jedi Masters such as Mace Windu, Plo Koon, Kit Fisto and Yoda. As with all Jedi, he was taken from a very young age to be trained as Jedi, spending he younger years as a youngling in the Jedi temple, being instructed in the Jedi way and the way of the force. He was then taken as an apprentice under Jedi master Qui-gon Jin. He followed Qui-gon Jin as he was tasked as mediator and peace keeper, learning in the shadow of a Jedi master. Obi-wan excelled in light saber combat.
