Obi-wan Kenobi

Jedi master and member of the Jedi High counsel, master to Anakin Skywalker, apprentice to Qui-gon Jin, general for the republic during the Clone Wars and mentor to Luke Skywalker.

Obi-wan Kenobi the wise and stoic man of honor and upholder of the Jedi code has been involved in many of the major conflicts seen throughout Star Wars, most of which took place during the clone wars of which he was a commanding general of the clone army. He also held a seat on the Jedi high counsel with other Jedi Masters such as Mace Windu, Plo Koon, Kit Fisto and Yoda. As with all Jedi, he was taken from a very young age to be trained as Jedi, spending he younger years as a youngling in the Jedi temple, being instructed in the Jedi way and the way of the force. He was then taken as an apprentice under Jedi master Qui-gon Jin. He followed Qui-gon Jin as he was tasked as mediator and peace keeper, learning in the shadow of a Jedi master. Obi-wan excelled in light saber combat.
